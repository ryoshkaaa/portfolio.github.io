According to the results of the competition, at the city stage I took 3rd place, and at the regional stage I received a special and unique prize from the jury for the best speech

MIPT summer project school "Hello, Russia" participant and scholarship winner

In the spring of this year, I learned about a grant competition for a two-week summer trip to a project school at a technopark, organized by Rossotrudnichestvo and MIPT. I immediately applied. To participate, I needed to write a motivational essay and submit my portfolio, which I did. I knew there would be many applicants and fierce competition, so imagine my joy when, a month later, I found out that I had won the grant and could attend completely free of charge!

After the trip, I was left with my own project, many new friends from all over the world, photos, and most importantly, knowledge and skills in various fields. During those two weeks, I not only learned everything about wind energy and how to work in 3D, but I also met renowned Russian scientists, attended their lectures, and tried a variety of activities, such as clay sculpting and making cotton candy. I also got to explore the capital of my homeland.

In addition, by the end of the summer school i won a competition "Best media product" with my creative video i made during the camp.
